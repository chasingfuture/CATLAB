About CATLAB

Makerspaces are places where people of many backgrounds and varying levels of experience can come together to learn, design, and build. While such spaces exist at the University of California, San Diego, none are completely open to all students and organizations on campus. CATLab is to be different. In partnering with Sixth College's Culture, Art, and Technology program, CATLab is planned to be the first community makerspace. In short, it will be a place for all creative minds at UCSD.
